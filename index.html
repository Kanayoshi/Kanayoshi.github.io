About me

I study arithmetic geometry. In particular, I am interested in the local-global principle (Hasse principle) and related topics.
I'm also interested in continued fractions and related topics, arithmetic statistics and so on.

I was a Ph.D. student in Department of Mathematics at Keio University, advised by Kenichi Bannai. I got a Ph.D in March, 2024. From April 2024, I work for a private company.
