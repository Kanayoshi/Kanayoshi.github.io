About me

I'm a Ph.D. student in Department of Mathematics at Keio University, advised by Kenichi Bannai.

I'm a part-time researcher at RIKEN AIP and JSPS Research Fellowship for Young Scientists (DC2) (Both April 2022~).

I study arithmetic geometry. In particular, I am interested in the local-global principle (Hasse principle) and related topics. I'm also interested in continued fractions and related topics, arithmetic statistics and so on.
